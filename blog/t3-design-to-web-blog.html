What is a responsive site? Why is responsiveness important?

Responsive sites are designed to adapt to the many differing screen sizes used to access the internet today. It is important that information on a website is as easy to access on a mobile or tablet as it is on a desktop.

What is mobile-first design? Why is it important

As websites are being accessed through many differing screen sizes, designers often build their websites to fit a mobile screen first. This is because it would be more difficult to adapt a webiste designed for a large screen to fit a small one than vice-versa.
