Self Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self-awareness?

Self-awareness and mindfulness are similar in that they both involve looking at, and understanding yourself without judgement. The clarity that mindfullness gives can maybe make it easier to observe yourself clearly.

What did you take away fromt he "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

That emotions are fleeting. They come and go. Emotions do not define us. If we experience sadness, we are not sad. We just feel sad at that point in time and it will pass.
